Description

From Fear is a 1 to 4 player survival horror where you choose your fear. In this post-apocalyptic world, you must scavenge to survive with your deepest fear lurking in the shadows. Do not believe everything you see.

About

From Fear is a co-op horror game by Tom Tjemsland. The demo was released 26th of October 2021 and early access is currently in development, with a planned release date of Q2/Q3 2022.

Features

  • Choose your fear and let the game adapt to your choice.
  • Unpredictable AI with look and skills based on your fear.
  • Different and unique levels to scavenge for loot.
